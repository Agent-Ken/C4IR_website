    About us

    C4IR (Center for Analysis and Coordination of the Fourth Industrial Revolution)

    Center for Analysis and Coordination of the Fourth Industrial Revolution was established under the Ministry of Economy in accordance with the Decree of the President of the Republic of Azerbaijan dated January 6, 2021. Center for Analysis and Coordination of the Fourth Industrial Revolution (C4IR) carries out cooperation between the Republic of Azerbaijan and international organizations operating in the field of the Fourth Industrial Revolution, as well as analyses and coordinates challenges, initiatives, strategies and projects related to digital economy. The main objective of the entity is to make the most of the opportunities created by the Fourth Industrial Revolution and to ensure the leading role of our country in the field of emerging technologies.

    From April 1, 2021, C4IR also undertakes the functions of the Azerbaijan Center for the Fourth Industrial Revolution Network of the World Ecomic Forum.

    Our mission:

    In order to make the most of the opportunities created by the Fourth Industrial Revolution and to strengthen the position of our country in this field, to carry out cooperation and coordination with local and international institutions, as well as the analysis and coordination of strategies and projects on the digital economy

    Our vision:

    To support the development of the digital economy and ensure that our country benefits from the technologies of the Fourth Industrial Revolution and ranks among the leading countries in this field
