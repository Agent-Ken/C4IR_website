    About us

    C4IR (Center for Analysis and Coordination of the Fourth Industrial Revolution)

    Center for Analysis and Coordination of the Fourth Industrial Revolution was established under the Ministry of Economy in accordance with the Decree of the President of the Republic of Azerbaijan dated January 6, 2021. Center for Analysis and Coordination of the Fourth Industrial Revolution (C4IR) carries out cooperation between the Republic of Azerbaijan and international organizations operating in the field of the Fourth Industrial Revolution, as well as analyses and coordinates challenges, initiatives, strategies and projects related to digital economy. The main objective of the entity is to make the most of the opportunities created by the Fourth Industrial Revolution and to ensure the leading role of our country in the field of emerging technologies.

    From April 1, 2021, C4IR also undertakes the functions of the Azerbaijan Center for the Fourth Industrial Revolution Network of the World Ecomic Forum.

    Our mission:

    In order to make the most of the opportunities created by the Fourth Industrial Revolution and to strengthen the position of our country in this field, to carry out cooperation and coordination with local and international institutions among other things.

    Our vision:

    To participate, support and improve the development of the digital economy and ensure that our country benefits from all the technologies of the Fourth Industrial Revolution and ranks among the leading countries in this field. That is our vision.

    Our Articles

    Artifical Intelligence

    Artificial intelligence is the field of science that allows machines and technologies to imitate humans. In other words, Artificial Intelligence can be thought of as a programmed machine simulation of the natural intelligence required to learn and imitate human actions. Machines with this intelligence can learn through experience and perform tasks in a human-like manner.

    Machine Learning

    Machine learning (ML) is a process of computer learning without human intervention and ML is a sub-section of AI. This type of statistical learning can be supervised or unsupervised. Machine learning algorithms are usually written based on mathematical models.

    Internet of Things

    The concept of the Internet of Things is the interaction of smart devices with each other. The large volume of data collected by this technology is managed by means of Big Data (Big Data) programs.

    Big Data

    The word Big Data has been used since the 1990s. This word was rarely used in the last century. However, recently, Big Data is used so much that our era is also called the "Data Era". Here, data is analyzed and classified, made meaningful and actionable.

    Blockchain

    The Blockchain technology, we often hear along with cryptocurrencies, is actually a type of database. Blockchain technology is a special system that stores data and it is very difficult - even impossible to manipulate or hack it. The blockchain is basically a "ledger" that records transactions that span the entire computer network.

    Robots

    Robots are intelligent machines that act intelligently depending on mechanical systems, control systems and computer algorithms. The main interest in developing robots is to teach them the ability to think and act like humans and even perform processes that are too difficult for humans. Today, the service of robots is widely used in many fields, such as healthcare, defense, education and industry.

    Drones

    In recent years, as a result of the evolution of technology and control systems, the demand for unmanned aerial vehicles (drones) has increased. Currently, agriculture, healthcare, education, art, media, construction, ecology, sports, etc. these devices are widely used in the fields. Drones are widely used in the military industry and civilian fields and drones are mainly used in reconnaissance, searching for missing people, victims of natural disasters and fugitive criminals, as well as photographing road accidents or traffic jams and so on.

    Virtual Reality & Augmented Reality

    Virtual reality and augmented reality are among the latest technological discoveries that serve a wide range of users in our modern world. Virtual reality is an AI based technology that allows us to take vision from 3D virtual world instead of taking them from 2D screens. Augmented reality allows us to integrate events from the virtual world into the real world using the object recognition and detection features of devices.

    Cloud Technology

    Cloud technology means storing and processing data on other people’s computers in a data center via a network that gives companies the ability to store massive amounts of data and process it in nearly real-time. Cloud service providers such as Amazon, Google, and Microsoft allow companies to host all vital IT infrastructure in their cloud rather than within an organization's digital walls reducing overhead costs to maintain and operate individual systems, software and data.
